# Tonge Customer Guide

**Welcome to Tonge — Speak Every Tongue.**

Tonge is an AI-powered language learning platform. Learn French, Spanish, Portuguese, Italian, German, English, Chinese, Japanese, Korean, Russian, Arabic, or Hindi — with a personal AI coach, smart flashcards, grammar references, and more.

## Getting Started

### Creating a Free Account

1. Go to **tonge-app.fly.dev**
2. Click **Get Started Free**
3. Enter your email address
4. Click **Continue** — no password needed
5. You're in. Free accounts get:
   - Full access to all 11 language reference guides
   - 5 AI Coach messages per day
   - Flashcards (unlimited)
   - Grammar, Vocabulary, Dialogues, Roadmap

### Upgrading to Premium

Premium removes the AI Coach daily limit and unlocks Streaks tracking.

1. Click **Account** → **Upgrade to Premium**
2. Choose Monthly ($9/mo) or Yearly ($79/yr — save 27%)
3. Complete payment via Stripe
4. You'll receive an **access code** by email (format: `TG-XXXXXXXX`)
5. Enter your code on the login screen to activate premium

### Logging In with an Access Code

1. Go to the app
2. Click **I have an access code**
3. Enter your `TG-XXXXXXXX` code
4. Click **Login**

Your code works on up to **2 devices simultaneously**.

### Forgot Your Access Code?

Go to the **Resend Code** page, enter your email, and your code will be emailed to you within seconds.

## Choosing Your Languages

When you first open the app (or click the language selector at the top), you'll see two panels:

**I AM LEARNING** — Pick the language you want to learn. This sets the target language for all content, AI Coach, and exercises.

**MY REFERENCE LANGUAGES** — Pick up to 3 languages you already know. The app uses these for translations, grammar explanations, and example sentences. English is selected by default.

Click **Done** when ready.

## The Tabs

### Quick Reference
A cheat sheet of the most essential phrases, grammar rules, and vocabulary for your target language. Great for beginners or a quick refresher.

### AI Coach
Your personal AI language tutor. Choose from:
- **Fill in the blank** — complete sentences with the correct word
- **Translation** — translate sentences from your reference language
- **Role play** — have a conversation in your target language
- **Vocabulary** — learn new words in context
- **Grammar drill** — practice a specific grammar point

Type your answer, submit, and get instant feedback with corrections and explanations. The AI adapts to your chosen language and learning goal.

> Free accounts: 5 messages per day. Premium: unlimited.

### Grammar
A structured reference of grammar topics for your target language — tenses, pronouns, prepositions, sentence structure, and more. Each topic includes a clear explanation and example sentences with audio.

### Structures
Common sentence patterns and structures used in everyday conversation. Learn how native speakers actually construct sentences, not just textbook rules.

### Vocabulary
Themed vocabulary lists — food, travel, business, emotions, and more — with translations and audio pronunciation for every word. Tap any word to hear it spoken.

### Drills
Rapid-fire practice exercises to reinforce vocabulary and grammar. Rate yourself on each item — the app tracks which areas need more work.

### Dialogues
Real-world conversations between native speakers. Read both sides, listen to the audio, and practice speaking along. Great for building natural conversation flow.

### Roadmap
A personalised learning path showing exactly what to study and when. Divided into phases (Survival → Fluency) with clear milestones, daily time commitments, and what you'll be able to do at each stage.

### Word Space
Save new words you encounter anywhere — from the AI Coach, vocabulary lists, or your own study. Add the word, its meaning, pronunciation, and an example sentence.

### Flashcards
Spaced repetition flashcards using the SM-2 algorithm — the same method used by Anki and language learning professionals. Cards you know well are shown less often; harder cards come back sooner.

**How to use:**
1. Tap a card to reveal the answer
2. Rate yourself: **Again** (didn't know it), **Hard**, **Good**, or **Easy**
3. The algorithm schedules the next review automatically

**Practice Again:** After completing your daily session, tap **Practice All Cards Again** to run through the full deck a second time — useful when you want extra repetition on words you're solidifying.

Cards are added automatically when you tap **Save to Flashcards** in the AI Coach or Word Space.

## Streaks

Streaks track your daily practice consistency. Each day you use the AI Coach or rate flashcards, your streak increases by 1. Missing a day resets it to zero.

> Streaks require a Premium subscription.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Can I learn multiple languages at once?**  
Yes — just switch the "I am Learning" language at the top. Your flashcards, progress, and AI Coach history are separate for each language.

**Does the AI Coach remember previous conversations?**  
No — each AI Coach session starts fresh. This is by design; it keeps responses focused and avoids confusion.

**Can I use Tonge offline?**  
The grammar, vocabulary, and dialogue content loads from a cache and may be available briefly offline. The AI Coach and audio require an internet connection.

**My audio isn't working — what do I do?**  
Make sure your device isn't muted. On iOS, check that the silent switch is off. Tap the ▶ button directly (not the word).

**I was charged but didn't get my access code — what do I do?**  
Check your spam folder first. If it's not there, go to the Resend Code page and enter the email you used for payment. If it still doesn't arrive, contact support.

**How do I cancel my subscription?**  
Go to Account → Manage Subscription → Cancel. Your access continues until the end of the current billing period.

**Can I get a refund?**  
We offer refunds within 7 days of purchase if you haven't found value in the product. Contact support with your email address.
