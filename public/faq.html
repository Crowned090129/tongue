Access Codes & Login
I lost my access code. How do I get it again? ▾

Go to /resend-code, enter the email you used to subscribe, and your current code will be resent immediately.

Also check your spam/junk folder for an email from Tonge.

My access code isn't working. What do I do? ▾

Common reasons a code won't work:

  • Expired: Monthly codes expire after 31 days. Check your email for the most recent renewal email.
  • Typo: The code is case-insensitive but must be entered exactly (e.g. TG-AB12CD34).
  • Subscription cancelled: If payment failed or you cancelled, the code is deactivated.
  • Renewed: When your subscription renews, your old code is replaced with a new one. Check your latest email.

If none of these apply, use Resend my code or contact support.

Can I use the app on multiple devices? ▾

Yes — you can be logged in on up to 2 devices simultaneously with the same access code. For example, your phone and your laptop.

Each subscription is for one person. Access codes are personal and must not be shared.

I keep getting logged out / "session expired" — why? ▾

Three reasons this might happen:

  • Subscription renewed: When your billing cycle renews, a new code is issued. The old code is deactivated. Check your email for the new one.
  • Code expired: Codes expire at the end of your billing period. After renewal, log in with the new code.
  • A third device logged in: The system supports 2 devices. If a third device logs in, the oldest session is ended.

If you see a yellow "Subscription renewed" banner on the login screen, that's expected — just enter your new code from the email.

Features & Learning
What is "Today's Practice" and how does it work? ▾

Today's Practice is a daily recommendation shown at the top of the Quick Reference tab. It suggests a different focus each day of the week:

  • Monday — New vocabulary words
  • Tuesday — Grammar study
  • Wednesday — Translation exercises
  • Thursday — Speaking drills
  • Friday — Conversation role-play
  • Saturday — Free writing practice
  • Sunday — Flashcard review

Tap the button to jump directly to the recommended feature. It's a suggested starting point to keep your learning varied and balanced — you don't have to follow it.

How does the AI Coach work? ▾

The AI Coach generates personalised exercises tailored to your target language and reference languages. Choose from 7 exercise types:

  • New Word — vocabulary with pronunciation, memory tips, and examples
  • Fill the Gap — complete a sentence in your target language
  • Translate — translate from your reference language
  • Give Opinion — express a view on a topic
  • Free Writing — write anything; AI corrects it with explanations
  • Mini Quiz — multiple-choice grammar or vocabulary
  • Role Play — respond to a real-life conversation scenario

Every exercise is unique, generated on demand, and graded with detailed feedback.

Free users get 5 AI messages per day. Premium users get up to 300 per day.

How do Flashcards and spaced repetition work? ▾

Tonge uses the SM-2 spaced repetition algorithm (the same as Anki) to schedule flashcard reviews:

  • New cards are shown on day 1
  • After rating a card "Good", it comes back in 6 days, then 15, then 35…
  • "Again" resets the card; "Easy" extends the interval faster
  • You only review cards exactly when you're about to forget them — maximally efficient

To add cards: in the Flashcards tab, tap "+ Add Card" or import words directly from Word Space. In the AI Coach, any "New Word" exercise has a "Save to Flashcards" button.

The Grammar / Quick Reference shows a "Generate" button — is that normal? ▾

Yes, that's by design. French has a hand-built grammar library with 500+ vocabulary cards and 27 grammar topics. For all other languages, content is AI-generated on demand and personalised to your reference languages.

Click "Generate" and wait ~15 seconds. The content is then saved in your browser — you'll never have to wait again for that language. If you want fresh content, use the "↺ Regenerate" button.

How do streaks work? ▾

Every day you complete any exercise (AI Coach, Drills, Flashcards), your streak count increases by 1. Miss a day and the streak resets to zero. Your longest streak ever is also tracked.

Your streak is displayed in the app header. Consistency — even just 15 minutes a day — is the most important factor in language learning.

What is Word Space? ▾

Word Space is an AI-powered translator and vocabulary builder. Type any word or phrase — in your target language, reference language, or even a mix — and get:

  • Translation in your target and reference languages
  • Phonetic pronunciation guide
  • Word-by-word breakdown (literal meaning of each part)
  • Grammar explanation (why is it structured this way?)
  • 4 real-life example sentences with voice
  • Comparison to your reference language

Tap "+ Save" to add words to your saved list. Tap "Reload" to expand any saved word again.

The pronunciation (▶ button) sounds wrong / robotic — how do I fix it? ▾

Pronunciation uses your device's built-in text-to-speech (TTS). If it sounds wrong, your device doesn't have a voice installed for the target language.

Fix: Tap Voice in the app header. The Voice Settings panel will show whether you have voices for your language and give exact steps to install them for your device (iPhone, Android, Mac, Windows).

After installing, restart your browser and reopen the app.

Subscription & Billing
How do I cancel my subscription? ▾

You can cancel anytime in two ways:

  1. Open the app → tap Account (top right) → Manage Billing → Cancel subscription in the Stripe portal.
  2. Or go directly to billing.stripe.com, enter your email, and cancel from there.

After cancellation, your access code stays active until the end of your current billing period. You will not be charged again.

Can I get a refund? ▾

We offer a 7-day refund for first-time subscribers if you're not satisfied. Email us at support@tonge.app within 7 days of your first payment.

Renewal charges are non-refundable. See our Terms of Service for full details.

Can I switch from monthly to yearly (or vice versa)? ▾

Yes. Open the app → tap Account → Manage Billing. In the Stripe billing portal, click "Update plan" to switch between monthly and yearly.

The change takes effect at your next billing cycle.

My payment failed. What do I do? ▾

If a payment fails, Stripe retries automatically over the next few days. You'll also receive an email with instructions.

To update your card: Open the app → Account → Manage Billing → Update payment method.

If all retries fail, your subscription is cancelled and your access code deactivated. You can resubscribe anytime at /subscribe.

How do I delete my account? ▾

Open the app → tap Account (top right) → scroll to the bottom → tap Delete my account. This will:

  • Cancel any active Stripe subscriptions immediately
  • Deactivate and delete all access codes
  • Anonymise your email address (GDPR erasure)

This action is permanent and cannot be undone.

Technical
Does it work offline? ▾

Partially. The app shell and previously-visited pages load offline. However, AI exercises, Word Space, and generating language content all require an internet connection (they call the AI API in real-time).

Flashcards and previously generated Grammar/Vocabulary tabs work offline since they're cached in your browser.

Is my data safe? Is the AI API key exposed? ▾

Yes, your data is secure. Key facts:

  • The Anthropic AI API key is stored only on the server — it is never sent to your browser.
  • All AI requests go through our server as an authenticated proxy.
  • Passwords are never stored — only hashed tokens.
  • We do not store credit card numbers — all billing is handled by Stripe.
  • You can delete your account and all data at any time.

See our Privacy Policy for full details.

I'm seeing "Too many requests" — what does that mean? ▾

The AI proxy allows up to 30 requests per minute per account. This prevents abuse and keeps the service fast for everyone.

If you hit the limit, wait 60 seconds and continue. In normal use — even heavy study sessions — you won't hit this limit.
