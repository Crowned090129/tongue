Privacy Policy

Tonge · Last updated: May 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Tonge ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our service.

1. What We Collect

  • Email address — provided during checkout, used to deliver your access code and billing communications.
  • Subscription and billing data — your subscription plan, status, and payment history, managed by Stripe. We do not store credit card numbers.
  • Access codes and session data — we generate and store access codes and session identifiers tied to your subscription.
  • Usage data — we log server-side requests (timestamps, endpoints, anonymised IP addresses) for security monitoring and debugging. We do not store the content of your AI conversations.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide and operate the Tonge service.
  • To send you your access code and renewal notifications by email.
  • To process payments and manage your subscription through Stripe.
  • To detect and prevent abuse (rate limiting, session enforcement, security monitoring).
  • To comply with legal obligations.

3. Third-Party Services

  • Stripe (stripe.com) — handles all payment processing. Subject to Stripe's Privacy Policy.
  • Resend (resend.com) — delivers transactional emails. Subject to Resend's Privacy Policy.
  • Anthropic (anthropic.com) — your prompts and responses are processed by Anthropic's Claude API. Subject to Anthropic's Privacy Policy. We do not store or log your AI conversation content on our end.

4. International Data Transfers

We and our third-party service providers (Stripe, Resend, Anthropic) may process your personal data in countries other than your own, including the United States, which may have different data protection laws than your country. By using the Service, you acknowledge this transfer. Where required, we rely on appropriate safeguards (such as standard contractual clauses) to protect your data during international transfers.

5. Data Retention

We retain your email address and subscription records for as long as your account exists, plus any additional period required by law or for legitimate business purposes (e.g., resolving billing disputes). You may request deletion of your account and associated personal data at any time by contacting us — see Section 9.

6. Data Security

Your data is stored in a secured, encrypted database. API keys and secrets are stored server-side and are never exposed to your browser. Access to subscriber data requires authentication. We apply industry-standard measures to protect your data, though no system is 100% secure.

7. Children's Privacy

The Service is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately and we will delete it promptly.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access — request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction — request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Erasure — request deletion of your personal data ("right to be forgotten").
  • Restriction — request that we limit how we process your data.
  • Portability — receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Objection — object to processing based on legitimate interests.
  • Withdraw consent — where processing is based on consent, you may withdraw it at any time.
  • Lodge a complaint — EU/UK residents may lodge a complaint with their local supervisory authority (e.g., the ICO in the UK, or your national data protection authority in the EU).

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at support@tonge.app. We will respond within 30 days.

9. California Residents (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), including the right to know what personal information we collect, the right to delete it, and the right to opt out of the sale of personal information. We do not sell your personal information. To exercise your rights, contact us at support@tonge.app.

10. Cookies and Local Storage

We do not use tracking or advertising cookies. We use browser localStorage to store your session token locally on your device. This data stays on your device and is never sent to third parties for advertising purposes.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you by email if changes are material. The updated policy will be effective as of the date posted.

12. Contact

Questions or privacy requests? Email us at support@tonge.app. We aim to respond within 30 days.
